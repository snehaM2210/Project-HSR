About the Journal and Editorial Board

    International Journal of Fuzzy Mathematical Archive (IJFMA) is an open access, peer reviewed and refereed journal devoted to publication of original research articles as well as review articles, with emphasis on unsolved problems and open questions in all branches of fuzzy mathematics, soft set theory, approximation theory, etc.

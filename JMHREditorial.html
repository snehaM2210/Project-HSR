About the Journal and Editorial Board

    Journal of Management and Humanity Research (JMHR) is a scholarly journal that publishes high quality scientific research on the theoretical development and practice of management and humanity, focusing on the problems, interest, and concerns of management, the behaviors and psychology advances of humans, as well as the co-evolution between the two disciplines. Within its scope are all aspects of knowledge related to management functional areas, humanity issues and inter- disciplines.

    Topics covered in journal include:

    Business Strategy

    New media and Marketing

    Supply Chain Management

    Technology Innovation

    Knowledge Management

    Optimization and Modeling

    Information Systems

    Psychology

    Sociology

    Education

    Cross-Culture studies

    Ecosystem

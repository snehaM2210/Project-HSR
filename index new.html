Welcome to House of Scientific Research

House of Scientific Research (HSR) is a publisher of peer-reviewed and open access journals covering a wide range of academic disciplines. The main aim of HSR is to develop highest quality knowledge-based products and service for the scientific communities all over the world. We invite new journal proposals for wide publication of scientific research in any fields of science. Also we intive Editor-in-Chief position for new journals.

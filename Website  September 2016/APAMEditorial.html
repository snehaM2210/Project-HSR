About the Journal and Editorial Board

    Annals of Pure and Applied Mathematics (APAM) is an open access, peer reviewed and refereed journal devoted to publication of original research articles as well as review articles, with emphasis on unsolved problems and open questions in all branches of Pure and Applied Mathematics.
